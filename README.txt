This repository includes the appendix, tables, and figures of a manuscript submitted to the 12th International Conference on Complex Networks and their Applications. The title of the manuscript is "Modeling the association between physician risky-prescribing and the complex network structure of physician shared-patient relationships".

Another file is the Supplementary Appendix of a paper accepted by the ANS Special Issue titled "Estimating the impact of physician risky-prescribing on the network structure underlying physician shared-patient relationships".
